Museum Typography

The aim of this project was to create a signage system for the Cambridge Museum of Technology that pays respect to the architecture and content of the site. This unusual lettering design appears to be unique to Cambridge and is worth preserving. I believe the signs harmonise with the building and help shed a little light on this history. The aim of any signage system is to help visitors find their way around a site and get the maximum enjoyment from their visit.
